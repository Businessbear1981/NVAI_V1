# Kiki Exposé PDF Analysis

- **Background:** Black with purple/magenta borders
- **Typography:** Magenta, cyan, and white text on black
- **Content:** 
  - Comprehensive biography with 10 key life chapters
  - Historical photos and artwork
  - Quotes from Hemingway, Cocteau, Man Ray
  - Artistic portraits (Modigliani, Corbellini, etc.)
  - Information on "Le Violon d'Ingres" ($12.4M photograph)

  - 700+ curated research links

## Strengths

- Comprehensive research
