# NVAI Artist Wing Room Design Concepts

## Foyer: The Garden Party
- **Aesthetic:** Napa Valley Chateau (Chateau Montelena style), Tuscan/French Riviera vibes.
- **Visuals:** Rolling hills, vineyards, golden sunset, stone chateau entrance.
- **Sound:** Ambient garden party chatter, clinking glasses, soft acoustic guitar.
- **Interaction:** Grand wooden doors that swing open to reveal the gallery.

## Wing 1: Picasso - Le Bateau-Lavoir (1901)
- **Aesthetic:** Montmartre tenement studio, Blue Period.
- **Visuals:** Candlelit gloom, peeling wallpaper, monochromatic blue lighting.
- **Sound:** Distant Parisian street sounds, dripping water, soft melancholic piano.
- **Carousel:** Circular "dry cleaner belt" rotation of 1901 works.

## Wing 2: Picasso - The Mediterranean Atelier (1934-1967)
- **Aesthetic:** Sun-drenched South of France studio.
- **Visuals:** Terracotta tiles, white walls, bright natural light, pottery artifacts.
- **Sound:** Cicadas, distant waves, upbeat Spanish guitar.
- **Carousel:** Rotating display of late-period works.

## Wing 3: Chagall - The Floating Dream
- **Aesthetic:** Dreamlike, ethereal space.
- **Visuals:** Deep ultramarine walls, stained-glass light effects, floating motifs.
- **Sound:** Klezmer-inspired violin, ethereal ambient pads.
- **Carousel:** Circular rotation of Chagall's career-spanning works.

## Wing 4: Modigliani & Kiki - Café de Montparnasse
- **Aesthetic:** 1920s Parisian café (Café du Dôme / La Rotonde).
- **Visuals:** Velvet curtains, smoke-filled atmosphere, phonographs, jazz posters.
- **Sound:** 1920s jazz, phonograph crackle, café chatter.
- **Special Feature:** "Salle de projection" for the Kiki short film.

## Wing 5: The Italian Masters - The Renaissance Studiolo
- **Aesthetic:** Florentine workshop / Sforza court cabinet.
- **Visuals:** Stone arches, ribbed-vault ceilings, sepia drafting paper, candlelight.
- **Sound:** Choral music, scratching of quills on parchment.
- **Carousel:** Rotating display of Da Vinci and Raphael pieces.

## Wing 6: Matisse - The Paper Cutout Chapel
- **Aesthetic:** Late Matisse in Vence (Chapelle du Rosaire).
- **Visuals:** Bright white walls, bold primary color cutouts, stained-glass light.
- **Sound:** Soft liturgical chanting, birdsong.
- **Carousel:** Circular rotation of Matisse's late works.

## Wing 7: Kandinsky - The Bauhaus Lab
- **Aesthetic:** Weimar Bauhaus / Modernist lab.
- **Visuals:** Geometric abstraction, primary colors, clean white lines.
- **Sound:** Schoenberg string quartet, mechanical clicks.
- **Carousel:** Rotating display of Kandinsky's abstract works.

## Wing 8: Frida Kahlo - Casa Azul
- **Aesthetic:** Vibrant Mexican sanctuary.
- **Visuals:** Cobalt blue walls, lush tropical plants, pre-Columbian artifacts.
- **Sound:** Traditional Mexican folk music, tropical birds.
- **Carousel:** Rotating display of Kahlo's personal works.

## Wing 9: Monet - The Giverny Pavilion
- **Aesthetic:** Impressionist garden / Conservation lab.
- **Visuals:** Water lilies, Japanese bridge, soft pastel light, conservation equipment.
- **Sound:** Water rippling, soft wind through leaves.
- **Carousel:** Rotating display of Monet's late garden works.
