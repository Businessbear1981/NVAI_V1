# NVAI Complete Vision & Context

## The Real Property: Villa Monticello (Napa Valley)
- 33-acre property surrounded by sprawling vineyards, mountain ranges, sunsets, moon rises
- Extremely remote and private
- Villa Monticello: Tuscan-inspired, built using castle stone
- Resort-style grounds with 4 decks, 4,500+ sq ft outdoor entertainment space
- Can handle groups up to 200 guests
- 15-minute drive to St. Helena, 20 minutes to Calistoga
- 30-minute drive to Jazzy Lady (44' Bayliner yacht)

## The Digital Experience Should Mirror This:
1. **Grand Courtyard Entry** - Open garden party setting (outdoor Napa ambiance)
2. **Garden Passage** - Arches with plants, flowers, weeping willows (Secret Garden aesthetic)
3. **Grand Foyer** - 75 ft ceilings, sweeping staircase, old-world French chateau with modern touches
   - Subway tile floor
   - Grand chandelier
   - Warm, museum-quality lighting
4. **Wings branching off** - Like rooms in the house:
   - Basement: Workshop/studio spaces (artist studios)
   - Upstairs: Artist galleries and collections
   - Each wing represents an artist's period/aesthetic
