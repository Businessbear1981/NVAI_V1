## Cover
# Napa Valley Art Institute (NVAI)
### Immersive Digital Gallery Experience Design Brief

## Slide 1
# The Vision: A Virtual Chateau Experience
- **Cinematic Immersion:** A high-end digital journey through a Napa Valley chateau.
- **Garden Party Foyer:** The entry point where guests arrive at a Tuscan-style estate.
- **Period-Specific Wings:** Each artist is featured in a room that reflects their historical context.
- **Interactive Discovery:** Rotating carousels and NDA-gated dossiers for deep engagement.

## Slide 2
# Three-Layer Platform Architecture
- **Layer 1: The Nervous System:** AI-driven APIs (Claude, Grok, Higgs Field, Eleven Labs) for content.
- **Layer 2: Business Logic:** Modular rooms, provenance workflows, and escrow/lending modules.
- **Layer 3: The Frontend:** A breathtaking UI/UX designed for cinematic impact and seamless flow.

## Slide 3
# The Foyer: Arrival at Chateau Montelena
- **Visual Aesthetic:** Rolling Napa hills, golden hour light, and ivy-draped limestone walls.
- **Atmosphere:** Ambient garden party sounds and the scent-suggestion of wisteria.
- **Interaction:** Grand wooden doors that swing open to reveal the gallery's interior.

## Slide 4
# Picasso: From Montmartre to the Mediterranean
- **Wing 1: Le Bateau-Lavoir (1901):** Candlelit Blue Period gloom and tenement studio grit.
- **Wing 2: The Atelier (1934-1967):** Sun-drenched South of France studio with terracotta and pottery.
- **Experience:** A circular "dry cleaner belt" carousel showcasing 11 cataloged works.

## Slide 5
# Modigliani & Kiki: 1920s Montparnasse
- **Aesthetic Anchor:** The smoke-filled jazz clubs and cafés of bohemian Paris.
- **Visual Language:** Velvet curtains, phonographs, and the "lost generation" atmosphere.
- **Marquee Feature:** A dedicated "Salle de Projection" for the Kiki de Montparnasse short film.

## Slide 6
# The Italian Masters: Renaissance Studiolo
- **Aesthetic:** A Florentine workshop for Da Vinci and Raphael's masterpieces.
- **Visuals:** Stone arches, ribbed-vault ceilings, and sepia drafting paper.
- **Atmosphere:** The smell of linseed oil and the sound of quills on parchment.

## Slide 7
# Monet: The Giverny Sanctuary
- **Aesthetic:** An Impressionist garden pavilion meeting a modern conservation lab.
- **Visuals:** Water lilies, Japanese bridges, and soft, dappled pastel light.
- **Honest Provenance:** A design that highlights the delicate nature and history of each piece.

## Slide 8
# Frida Kahlo: The Blue House (Casa Azul)
- **Aesthetic:** A vibrant, deeply personal sanctuary in Coyoacán.
- **Visuals:** Cobalt blue walls, Mexican folk art, and lush tropical greenery.
- **Sound:** Traditional Mexican folk music and the calls of tropical birds.

## Slide 9
# Interactive Features & Provenance Gates
- **Circular Carousels:** Rotating displays that allow users to select and feature pieces.
- **NDA-Gated Dossiers:** Secure access to full provenance, technical reports, and affidavits.
- **Escrow & Lending:** Integrated modules for transaction settlement and asset-backed lending.

## Slide 10
# Next Steps: From Design to Deployment
- **Frontend Prototype:** Building the playable chateau experience in FastAPI + React.
- **Asset Generation:** Using Higgs Field and Eleven Labs for immersive video and audio.
- **Backend Integration:** Wiring the business logic and AI-driven provenance narratives.
