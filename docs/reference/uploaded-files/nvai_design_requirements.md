# NVAI Design Brief - Key Visual Requirements

## Foyer: Arrival at Chateau Montelena
**Visual Aesthetic:**
- Rolling Napa hills with golden hour light
- Ivy-draped limestone walls
- Romantic, cinematic scene
- Wisteria flowers (purple cascading blooms)
- Grand ornate wooden doors with carved grape vine details
- Warm golden interior lighting visible through doors

**Atmosphere:**
- Ambient garden party sounds
- Subtle scent-suggestion of blooming wisteria
- Grand wooden doors that swing open to reveal gallery interior

**Interaction:**
- Doors open to invite discovery into the gallery

## Picasso Wing: From Montmartre to the Mediterranean
**Wing 1: Le Bateau-Lavoir (1901)**
- Candlelit Blue Period gloom
- Tenement studio grit aesthetic
- Dark, moody lighting

**Wing 2: The Atelier (1934-1967)**
- Sun-drenched South of France studio
- Terracotta and pottery
- Warm Mediterranean light

**Experience:**
- Circular "dry cleaner belt" carousel
- Showcases 11 cataloged works
- Period-appropriate lighting for each wing

## Architecture
**Three-Layer Platform:**
1. **Layer 1: Nervous System** - Claude, Grok, Higgs Field, Eleven Labs (AI-driven APIs)
2. **Layer 2: Business Logic** - Modular rooms, Provenance workflows, Escrow/lending
3. **Layer 3: Frontend** - Cinematic UI/UX, seamless flow

## Visual Style Direction
- **Gold/Amber accents** throughout
- **Warm, museum-quality lighting**
- **Cinematic immersion** as core aesthetic
- **Period-accurate room designs** for each artist
- **Wisteria and garden elements** in foyer
- **Ivy-draped stone walls** for Chateau aesthetic
- **Ornate wooden doors** with carved details
